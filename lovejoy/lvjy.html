Lovejoy

Lovejoy is a British indie band formed in Brighton, England in 2020. The band is composed of frontman, Internet personality, and guitarist Wilbur Soot, guitarist Joe Goldsmith, bassist Ash Kabosu, and drummer Mark Boardman. Lovejoy’s music carries British indie rock and comedy music influences, and their lyrics often center around loss, failed romance, and politics.
